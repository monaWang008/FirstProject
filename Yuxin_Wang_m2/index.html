Recipe of Chinese Yam & Spare Rib Soup

Yuxin Wang(Mona) WNM601


  • Ingredient
  • Steps
  • Reference

Ingredients

Pork Spare Rib

Chinese Yam

Carrot

Ginger

Green Onion

Cooking Wine

Salt

Steps

  1. Preparation

    2. wash clean pork spare rib

    cut Chinese Yam and carrot

    cut ginger and green onion


  2. Process

    3. boil pork spare rib into cold water and add 1 spoon cooking wine

    and fry it with a few ginger slice


  3. Boil

    4. put fried pork spare rib, Chinese Yam and carrot into boiling water

    cook 30 minutes by a big fire, and then turn a small fire one more 60-80 minutes


  4. Finish

    5. turn off the fire, add 2 spoon of salt and chopped green onion, enjoy the delicary!
